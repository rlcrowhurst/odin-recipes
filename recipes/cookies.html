White Chocolate Cookies

Recipe for gooey white chocolate cookies

These white chocolate cookies are a perfect simple bake that everyone in the family will enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 120g oftened unsalted butter
  • 85g light brown soft sugar
  • 65g golden caster sugar
  • 1 medium egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla bean paste
  • 180g plain flour
  • 1/2 tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • 180g white chocolate (broken in to chunks)

Steps

  1. Heat the oven to 180C and line two large baking sheets with parchment
  2. Beat the butter and sugars together in a large bowl using an electric whisk.
  3. Crack in the egg and beat again
  4. Stir through the vanilla, flour, bicarb, chocolate chunks and 1/4 tsp fine sea salt
  5. Roll the mixture into 10 even-sized balls between the palms of your hands. Arrange these over the prepared baking sheets and bake for 10-12 minutes until golden brown at the edges
  6. Leave to cool on the sheets, then transfer to a write rack
