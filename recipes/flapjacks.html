Golden Syrup Flapjacks

Chewy golden syrup flapjacks

Flapjacks are an ideal recipe to cook with the kids, or a quick treat to share with friends

Ingredients

  • 250g jumbo porridge oats
  • 125g butter
  • 125g light brown sugar
  • 2-3 tbsp golden syrup

Steps

  1. Head the oven to 200C
  2. Put the oats, butter, sugar and golden syrup in a food processor and pulse until mixed
  3. Lightly butter a 20 x 20cm baking tin and add the mixture. Press into the corners with the back of a spoon so the mixture is flat and score into 12 squares.
  4. Bake for around 15 mins until golden brown
